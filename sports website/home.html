About Cricket

Cricket is a sport that is played by two teams of 11 players each. It involves hitting a ball with a bat and running around the field to score runs. The game is usually played on a large oval field, with a 22-yard long rectangular pitch in the center. The objective is to score as many runs as possible in a set number of overs. The team that scores the most runs wins the game.
